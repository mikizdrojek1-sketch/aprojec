Collection

Products that combine technology, design and performance.

Our product selection focuses on durability, insulation, everyday comfort and premium finishing that perfectly suits both renovation and new-build projects.

01 — PVC system

Aluplast Ideal 7000 NL

Aluplast Ideal 7000 NL is a PVC profile system for windows and doors in a block design, specially developed for the Dutch market with an installation depth of 120 mm. The system offers very good thermal insulation, high rigidity and a classic appearance that resembles traditional wooden frames.

More information
02 — PVC system

Gealan S9000NL

Gealan S9000NL is a 120 mm block profile system developed for the Dutch market. Thanks to the 3-seal system (MD), it offers very high airtightness, strong thermal insulation and extra protection against wind and moisture. This system is often used in energy-efficient homes and renovation projects.

More information
03 — PVC system

REHAU Synego

REHAU Synego is a 120 mm PVC window system in block design, developed with a strong focus on comfort, durability and sound insulation. The refined REHAU construction, including the smooth HDF surfaces, provides a premium finish and an excellent balance between insulation and profile stability.

More information
04 — Aluminium system

Aluprof MB-79N

Aluprof MB-79N is an aluminium window and door system with a slim, modern look and very good insulating properties. It is a contemporary and economical solution suitable for both residential and commercial projects.

More information
05 — Shutter system

Adaptive shutter SK90

SK90 is an external add-on shutter with a clean, square 90° box design. This system is mainly intended for existing buildings and has a modern, robust appearance. Its major advantage is the easy installation without major modifications to the frame.

More information
06 — Sliding system

Smart Slide

Smart Slide is a PVC sliding door system that combines the benefits of PSK and HST. The system is light to operate, seals well and is more economical than many other sliding solutions. That makes it ideal as a terrace door with large glass surfaces.

More information
07 — Terrace system

HST

HST stands for lift-and-slide doors: large terrace doors where the door leaf is first lifted and then slides open smoothly. This system allows very large glass surfaces while also offering high airtightness, great ease of use and a luxurious appearance.
